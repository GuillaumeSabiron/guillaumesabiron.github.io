Featured work

From scientific systems to useful services

PLANET’AIR

From fragmented scientific models to a territorial decision platform

PLANET air is a SaaS decision-support platform for air quality and pollutant emissions. It can be deployed on any territory and provides road-traffic emissions and noise assessments from mobility data and models. My role covers the product and scientific roadmap, functional architecture, prioritisation, and coordination of scientific and software contributors.
